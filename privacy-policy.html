Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 25, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Kurukshetra InfoBot, an official WhatsApp-based information service provided by the District Administration of Kurukshetra, Haryana, India. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you interact with our WhatsApp chatbot.

2. Information We Collect

When you use Kurukshetra InfoBot, we may collect:

  • Phone Number: Your WhatsApp phone number (used only for responding to your queries)
  • Messages: The content of messages you send to the bot
  • Timestamp: Date and time of interactions
  • Usage Data: Which menu options you select (for improving service)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for:

  • Responding to your queries about district information, tourist places, and government services
  • Improving the bot's functionality and user experience
  • Maintaining service logs for technical troubleshooting
  • Compliance with legal obligations

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Storage Duration: Message logs are stored temporarily for 30 days and then automatically deleted
  • Security: All data is encrypted and stored on secure servers (Vercel)
  • Access: Only authorized district administration personnel have access to logs
  • No Permanent Storage: We do not maintain a permanent database of user conversations

5. Data Sharing

We do NOT share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • When required by law or legal process
  • With WhatsApp/Meta (as per their platform requirements for message delivery)
  • With Vercel (our hosting provider) for technical infrastructure

We never sell or rent your data to anyone.

6. Your Rights

Under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDPA), you have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with the Data Protection Board of India

To exercise these rights, contact us using the details below.

7. Children's Privacy

This service is intended for users aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18. If you are a parent/guardian and believe your child has used this service, please contact us.

8. Third-Party Services

Our bot uses the following third-party services:

  • WhatsApp Business Platform (Meta): For message delivery - WhatsApp Privacy Policy
  • Vercel: For hosting and infrastructure - Vercel Privacy Policy

9. Cookies and Tracking

Our WhatsApp bot does NOT use cookies or tracking technologies. We only process messages sent directly to us via WhatsApp.

10. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with the "Last Updated" date revised. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Compliance

This Privacy Policy complies with:

  • Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (India)
  • Information Technology Act, 2000
  • WhatsApp Business Platform Terms
